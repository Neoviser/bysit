Enemies

Below is a list of enemies you may encounter in Deepwoken:

Sharko: The sharko is the land shark, with legs and big claws that can kick you very far away.

Mudskipper: Mindless walking corpses consumed by a fungus, seeking to turn you into one too.

Bandits: Lawless people who steal and kill.

Stone Golem: A sophisticated creation that is hostile.

Knives of Elfis: Mercenaries paid to kill you.
